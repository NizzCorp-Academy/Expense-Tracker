Key Features

Everything you need to manage your finances effectively

User Authentication

Secure login with email/password or Google Sign-In via Firebase Auth to protect your financial data.

Transaction Management

Add, edit, and delete income/expense entries with ease. Keep track of every financial transaction.

Categorization

Organize your transactions into categories for better financial insights and budgeting.

Real-time Sync

Cloud Firestore ensures your data is synchronized across all devices in real-time.

Interactive Charts

Visualize your spending patterns with beautiful pie and bar charts for better financial analysis.

Offline Support

Firebase cache allows you to access and modify your data even without internet connection.
